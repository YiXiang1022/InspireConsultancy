About Us

Company Background

Inspire Consultancy is a dynamic and client-centric insurance provider, operating under the umbrella of Great Eastern, one of the leading insurance companies globally. Established in 2020 by visionary entrepreneur Bethany Hoo, Inspire Consultancy has quickly emerged as a trusted name in the insurance industry, offering a comprehensive range of insurance products tailored to meet the diverse needs of our valued clientele

  • An authorized agency under Great Eastern
  • Rapid Emergence as a Trusted Name
