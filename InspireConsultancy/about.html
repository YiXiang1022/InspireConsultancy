Our Background

Inspire Consultancy is a dynamic, customer-centric financial Consultancy, which representing Great Eastern Insurance Company. Founded in 2020 by the visionary entrepreneur Beth Hoo, it swiftly became a trusted brand in the insurance industry, offering a comprehensive range of financial and planning products to meet the diverse needs of our esteemed clients

  • Authorized Agency under Great Eastern
  • Rapid Ascension to Trustworthy Reputation
  • Customer-Centric Approach Providing One-Stop Financial Solutions
  • Proven Experience in Successful Claims

Inspire Consultancy was born out of a passion for delivering unparalleled insurance solutions coupled with a commitment to excellence. Bethany envisioned a company that would not only provide essential coverage but also foster enduring relationships built on trust, integrity, and personalized service. With this vision in mind, Inspire Consultancy was established to redefine the insurance experience, empowering individuals and businesses to safeguard their assets and secure their futures with confidence
