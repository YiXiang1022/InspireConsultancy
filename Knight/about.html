Our Background

Inspire Consultancy is a dynamic and client-centric insurance provider, operating under the umbrella of Great Eastern, one of the leading insurance companies globally. Established in 2020 by visionary entrepreneur Bethany Hoo, ABC has quickly emerged as a trusted name in the insurance industry, offering a comprehensive range of insurance products tailored to meet the diverse needs of our valued clientele

  • An authorized agency under Great Eastern
  • Rapid Emergence as a Trusted Name
  • Client-Centric Approach

Inspire Consultancy was born out of a passion for delivering unparalleled insurance solutions coupled with a commitment to excellence. Kris envisioned a company that would not only provide essential coverage but also foster enduring relationships built on trust, integrity, and personalized service. With this vision in mind, ABC was established to redefine the insurance experience, empowering individuals and businesses to safeguard their assets and secure their futures with confidence
